If you are managing a consulting practice, you are always challenged to put your best foot forward to win new customers and secure new contracts. As new challenges are uncovered, it is expensive to pull your consultants from billing hours to research and develop the needed collateral and practices. Wouldn't it be great to extend your consulting team using a verified expert, so that she can help guide your team? Wouldn't it also be great to be able to have access to a library of customizable collateral such as project plans and functional requirements? Caasbah enables consultant practices to extend their staff with proven expert assistance and a library of customizable collateral for all types of projects.

A Caasbah project...

  1. Create a project request.
  2. Browse and select resources such as project plans, functional requirements, and/or Caasbah kulus (members)
  3. When a Caasbah kulu is needed, qualified members will bid on your project.
  4. Review the bids and reputation of the kulu.
  5. Select appropriate kulu and begin project.
  6. Caasbah project management tools to keep tabs on the project
  7. Provide feedback on your project and kulu.

Are you an rockstar in your field? Do you keep finding projects that don't challenge you enough? By signing up as a Caasbah Guru you can find projects targeted to your skill set. Upon setting up a Caasbah account you will be entered into the Caasbah Gurukul for your skill set and be able to bid on new projects. To help assure the highest level of quality for the customer, you will be assigned a Guru, or mentor, to guide you through the process. During the course of the project, the guru and the customer will be asked to rate your performance. Upon successfully completing a project, you will receive Caasbah points that improve your rating and advance your journey towards becoming a Caasbah Guru.

FAQ

I am a IT manager or a Consulting Principal why should I choose Caasbah for my resources?
It is difficult to find expert resources to help with your projects. Caasbah uses a proprietary system to verify and validate the qualifications of its experts to ensure your project will have the best resource available. Also, Casbaah project management tools to ensure your project is handled in a professional way, without needing to invest in additional resources.
Why do you need funding for this project?
The founders of Caasbah have been working in the IT field for the last 18 years and have seen the challenges in running IT projects, consulting services, and finding projects that challenge them. Funding will go toward developing the first iteration of the site, and bootstrapping the Guru program.
I am an industry expert why should I sign up as a member?
As an expert in your field you have a unique skill set, that you should be able to leverage and use often. Sure you get referrals for projects from past projects, but wouldn't it be better to use your reputation to find more work and to be able to mentor others? With Caasbah you can choose the work you want to do and even make money for mentoring less experienced experts.
What will I get if help fund this project?
In this first phase of funding you will receive 5x the amount in funded in services to use at launch. If your an industry expert you will also be provided early access into the Guru program and be asked to provide feedback on the process.
How do you verify your Caasbah kulu members?
When a new members signs up they are put into a Caasbah Gurukul for the skill set. The member is asked to fill out a profile and provide details of their experiences. This profile is reviewed by Caasbah and then if the member is approved, their profile appears in the marketplace and they are able to bid on projects. Starting out the kulu will not have built a reputation, to help build their reputation and assure quality a Guru will be assigned to shepherd every project. During the course of the project, the guru and the customer will be asked to rate the kulu's performance. Upon successfully completing a project, the kulu will receive Caasbah points and customer provided testimonials that improve their rating and advance their journey towards becoming a Caasbah Guru.
